WhadDiDo?.io
A browser-based gaming platform built for fun, creativity, and accessibility — no downloads, no installs, just play.
▸ WELCOME

Welcome to our gaming website! This platform was created to bring together fun, creativity, and simple browser-based games that anyone can enjoy. Whether you want to relax, challenge yourself, or just pass some time — there's something here for everyone.

🎯
OUR MISSION

Make gaming easy and accessible. You shouldn't need a powerful computer or downloads to enjoy games — just open the website and start playing.

🕹️
WHAT'S HERE
  • ⚡ Fun browser games
  • 🧠 Simple & addictive challenges
  • ➕ New games added over time
  • ⏱️ Play anytime, instantly
👨‍💻
THE CREATOR
Sathvik

This website was created and developed by Sathvik, an independent developer and gaming enthusiast. What started as a small personal project turned into a platform where people can enjoy games directly from their browser.

Sathvik enjoys building things with code and experimenting with new ideas to make gaming more fun and accessible.
